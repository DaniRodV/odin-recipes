Ranch Chicken Tacos

Description

These ranch chicken tacos are a great change from regular Mexican-style tacos. A quick, cool summer dinner made with leftover rotisserie chicken. Naturally, you can use any type of chicken cooked the way you like; just be sure to shred it for real tacos.

Ingredients

  • Ranch dressing
  • Reduced-fat sour cream
  • Taco seasoning mix
  • Chunky salsa
  • Rotisserie chicken
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Black olives
  • Avocados
  • Colby-Monterrey Jack cheese

Preparation

  1. Prepare the dressing: Combine ranch dressing, sour cream, 1 teaspoon taco seasoning, and salsa in a small bowl. Cover and refrigerate until serving.
  2. Mix the chicken in: Toss chicken with remaining taco seasoning. Cover bowl loosely with wax paper or plastic wrap. Microwave chicken until chicken is heated through, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Make your tacos: Warm tortillas in a skillet for about a minute on each side to make them pliable. Place a scoop of chicken on the tortilla and top with lettuce, tomato, green onion, olives, avocado, cheese, and a spoonful of the ranch dressing mixture.
